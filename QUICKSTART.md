## ğŸ’¡ Tips for Success

1. **Consistency is Key**
   - Set realistic daily goals
   - Review every day to maintain your streak

2. **Use Authentic Content**
   - Import content that interests you
   - Start with easier texts, gradually increase difficulty

3. **Don't Skip Reviews**
   - The spaced repetition system is optimized for retention
   - Review cards when they're due for best results

4. **Engage with Multiple Skills**

5. **Take Breaks**
   - Learning is most effective in focused sessions
   - Quality over quantity
